**Critical Training Points to Emphasize:**

*   **Character Swapping:** The most common trick is replacing letters with look-alike numbers (`o`→`0`, `l`→`1`, `e`→`3`).
*   **Addition/Removal:** Adding extra characters (`whatsappp`) or removing them.
*   **Case Sensitivity:** Using capital letters in place of lowercase ones (`ubuntU`).
*   **Homograph Attacks:** Using similar-looking characters (`vmvvare` with 'vv' for 'w').
*   **Hyphen Tricks:** Adding or removing hyphens to create a convincing-looking domain.
*   **The Golden Rule:** Always scrutinize the domain name (the part after the `@`) letter by letter. If it's not **exactly** the official domain, it is fraudulent.

### **Slide 2 ‚Äî What is Data Privacy?**

**Data Privacy** = Protecting how data is collected, used, stored, and shared.
**Data Protection** = Technical + procedural safeguards that enforce privacy.

üì¶ Example:

* Privacy ‚Üí Deciding **who can access** your data.

### **Slide 5 ‚Äî Secure Data Handling Lifecycle**

**2Ô∏è‚É£ Storage:**
